About Ghostbar at PALMS Resort & Casino

Ghostbar is one of Las Vegas's most iconic nightlife destinations, perched on the 55th floor of PALMS Resort & Casino at 4321 W Flamingo Rd. Originally opened in 2001 and famously featured on MTV's "The Real World: Las Vegas," Ghostbar has defined rooftop nightclub culture in Nevada for over two decades.

Following renovations, Ghostbar Las Vegas is open in 2026 — operating Wednesday through Saturday inside the Ivory Tower of PALMS. The venue delivers panoramic 360° views of the Las Vegas Strip and valley from its glass-enclosed sky patio, along with a full indoor lounge, bottle service, and DJ entertainment.

How Much Is Ghostbar Bottle Service in Las Vegas?

GXP Tours offers Ghostbar bottle service at a flat $500 per bottle — all taxes, gratuity, and venue fees already included. With GXP Tours, the price you see is the final price. No surprises at the door.

How Do I Book Ghostbar Bottle Service?

Booking is done entirely online in under 2 minutes. Select your date, choose your bottle(s), and secure your table with a $99 deposit — the remaining balance isn't due until 7 days before your reservation. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately, and a GXP host will follow up with your full check-in details before your night.

Should I Book Ghostbar Bottle Service in Advance?

Yes — we strongly recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for Friday and Saturday nights, holiday weekends, and special events. Tables sell out early and cannot be held night-of. The $99 deposit locks in your table immediately and removes all uncertainty from your Vegas plans.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Call or email us anytime.

What is bottle service / table service?
Bottle service means you reserve a table at the nightclub rather than buying individual drinks at the bar. The price you pay is primarily for the reserved "real estate" — the table, seating, service, and VIP treatment — along with the bottle(s) of your choice.
What is a bottle presentation?
Waitresses bring your bottles to the table while carrying flashing celebratory lights and (on occasion) a celebration sign. They'll dance to the music before setting your bottles up. It's a high-energy moment that announces your arrival.
How long will my reservation last?
Around 3–5 hours depending on your arrival time. We recommend arriving 9:00–10:30 PM to maximize your time. Ghostbar typically closes around 2–3 AM depending on the night.
What if my group doesn't drink alcohol?
The price is about the table "real estate" you're reserving for the night, not just the bottles. You don't have to drink the alcohol, but the price remains the same because you're still getting the table area, service, the DJ, and the atmosphere for the evening.
What forms of ID are accepted at the door?
A non-expired, valid government-issued ID — driver's license, passport, or State ID. Clubs do not accept temporary or paper IDs, or photos of IDs on your phone. Every guest must bring a physical, valid ID.
What if someone in my group turns 21 at midnight that night?
That group member will need to wait until midnight before being allowed into the venue. We also recommend they bring a backup ID if their current ID expires that same day — which is common on 21st birthdays.
What if I have more than 100 people?
We can still help! Contact us directly at info@gxptours.com or call (702) 483-0463 and a GXP host will work with you on accommodating your larger group.
Can I order additional items at my table?
Yes. You may order additional menu items while at your table, but you'll need to pay the waitress directly since your bottle service bill has already been paid and closed before you arrive.
Does Ghostbar have food?
No, Ghostbar does not have a food menu. Please make sure to have dinner before arriving at the venue.
Is Ghostbar wheelchair accessible?
Yes. The venue is only accessible by elevator, which is ADA compliant. There are escalators and an elevator just off the main PALMS casino floor that take you up to the 55th floor.
Is Ghostbar Las Vegas open in 2026?
Yes — Ghostbar Las Vegas is open and operating in 2026. The venue runs Wednesday through Saturday nights at PALMS Resort & Casino, 4321 W Flamingo Rd. Some older listings online show Ghostbar as "closed" — those refer to a prior renovation period. Ghostbar has since reopened and is fully operational as of 2026.

No hidden fees, no bill at the end of the night. With GXP Tours, the price you see is the final price.

The remaining balance is due 7 days before your reservation date. A GXP host will follow up with your full check-in details and answer any remaining questions before your night.

Tables sell out early and can't be reserved the same night. The $99 deposit secures your table immediately and locks in your access before availability runs out.
What is the best table at Ghostbar?
The most popular tables are the indoor sections near the dance floor with direct views of the DJ booth and main stage — great for larger groups who want high energy. For a more scenic experience, the outdoor sky patio tables offer unobstructed 360° views of the Las Vegas Strip.
